Preparation:

Step 1

In a deep bowl, put the beef and seasoning with salt, pepper and vinegar. Mix, cover with plastic wrap and let stand.

Step 2

In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of oil. Add the beef, so that it has a baking. Remove the meat.

Step 3

In the same pan, cook the onion, tomatoes and cook 4 minutes. Add 1 tablespoon parsley and yellow pepper, season with salt and pepper.

Step 4

Add to the beef. Mix well and reserve hot while they prepare the chips. Rice accompany.
